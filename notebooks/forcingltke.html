Based on the above figures, We can see that Simulation 6 produces more cloud core fractions, and Simulation 7 is the closest one to the observation. What we learned today.Â¶

Based on them, I assume the different large-scale forcing lengths impact turbulence and thus cloud core fraction.Â¶

I know you all know this, but just to recap, TKE is the turbulent kinematic energy and thus it is the energy for turbulence from the surface to the atmospheric boundary layer. TKE = (U^2 + V^2 + W^2)/2Â¶
